enum as2transition::TransitionFormatter::Option::type
Enumerator
FMT 

General format option.

Pass "raw", "inner", "eql", or "short" as value. "raw" - Do not format predicates. "inner" - Strip h(), occ(), ab_h(), and ab_occ() "eql" - Write eql(c,v) as c=v "short" - Compress c=true and c=false to c and -c.

SHOW_ALMOST_ALL 

Show all predicates.

SHOW_ALL

SHOW_NEGATED 

show negated predicates (true, false).

SHOW_STRONG_NEGATED 

show strong negated predicates (true, false).

SHOW_CONTRIBS 

show contribution predicates (true, false).

SHOW_XPREDS

SEP_ACTIONS

SEP_ABS

STRIP_SAN
