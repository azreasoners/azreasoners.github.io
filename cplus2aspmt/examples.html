Examples

These are some basic examples with all intermediate as well as output files for each:

Thermostat

This is the example of a simple thermostat. The thermostat starts at some initial temperature. The goal is to obtain a plan to control the working of the thermostat so that the temperature is maintained at a certain level.

Water Tank

This example describes a water tank example with 2 tanks. These 2 tanks are controlled by taps flowing at some given rate. Additionally, the water in each trank drains at some constant rate .We apply the restriction that you can add water to only 1 tank at a time. The goal is to find a plan to control the flow of water in the tanks so that the level in both tanks remains above a certain level.

2 Ball

This example involves 2 balls with the same elasticity falling to the ground. Ball 1 starts at 2 units from the ground while ball 2 starts at 3 units from the ground. Our aim is to observe the transition system of these 2 balls with the passage of time.

Car(Straight Motion)

This is the example of the motion of a car moving along a straight path. The car may accelerate or decelerate to reduce speed. Assuming the car starts with a speed of 0 units, the goal is to obtain the plan of the speed and distance of the car given the final position of the car

Car(Turning Motion)

Similar to the previous example, this is the example of the motion of a car. This car however has the ability to turn left or right with some constant angle. Assuming the car starts at origin and there exists certain obstacles in the 2D space, the goal is to obtain the path of the car while evading the obstacles given the final position of the car
