Knowledge representation and reasoning is an important area of Artificial Intelligence (AI) concerned with how knowledge can be represented in formal semantics and how to automate reasoning on the encoded knowledge (knowledge base).

We conduct research in both "low level" and "high level". To give you the idea about the research that we are doing, I suggest you to take a close look at the following papers:

  • "Mathematical foundations of answer set programming," P. Ferraris and V. Lifschitz, in We Will Show Them! Essays in Honour of Dov Gabbay, Vol. 1, 2005, pp. 615-664.
  • "Nonmonotonic causal theories," E. Giunchiglia, J. Lee, V. Lifschitz, N. McCain and H. Turner, Artificial Intelligence, Vol. 153, 2004, pp. 49-104.
Take a look at additional papers.
