Cancellation Policy - Registration:

Requests for registration cancellation must be received in writing by the ICLP 2013 Registration Secretariat (DEKON) by fax at +90 212 347 63 63, or via e-mail to registration@iclp2013.org. An administrative fee of € 50 will be applied for any cancellation received before or on July 22, 2013.
Absolutely no refund will be processed for cancellations received on or after July 23, 2013.
Please note that registration refunds will be processed within 30 days after the end of ICLP 2013, bank charges will be deducted from the refund.
